About Anglican Girls' College Ketu - Ijanikin

Anglican Girls' College Ketu - Ijanikin is a faith-based secondary school established to provide quality education in a morally sound and disciplined environment. Our mission is to raise God-fearing leaders equipped with academic excellence and strong character.

Our Vision

To be one of Nigeria’s leading institutions for moral and academic excellence.

Our Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Faith
  • Integrity
  • Academic Excellence
  • Service to Humanity
