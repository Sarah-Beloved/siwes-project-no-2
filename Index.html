Why Anglican Girls' College Ketu - Ijanikin?

Anglican Girls’ College is a place where excellence meets discipline. We provide quality education in a peaceful and God-fearing environment, helping every student grow academically, morally, and spiritually. Our experienced teachers, modern facilities, and strong Christian values prepare our girls to become confident and successful leaders of tomorrow.
